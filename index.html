The Problem

AI is moving fast. Compliance frameworks are catching up. Most enterprises are caught in between.

⚠️

Regulatory Blind Spots

The EU AI Act and NIST AI RMF introduce new obligations most legal and compliance teams aren't equipped to interpret or operationalize within existing workflows.

🔒

Sensitive Data at Risk

AI systems in legal, health, and financial contexts routinely process privileged, protected, or confidential data — without purpose-built safeguards around how that data flows.

📊

No Audit Trail

When something goes wrong, enterprises lack documentation of how AI decisions were made — exposing them to legal, regulatory, and reputational consequences.

🧩

Fragmented Tooling

Existing compliance platforms weren't built for AI. Bolting on governance after the fact creates gaps, inefficiencies, and false confidence.
